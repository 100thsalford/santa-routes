When will you be on my street?

  • Unfortunately, we can't give exact times. Each route starts around 6pm and can run as late as 9pm. Traffic may mean we start late, and large crowds can slow us down en route. Please be patient, and follow the Facebook page as many people provide updates when they've seen Santa. Check the Map for a rough idea of the routes

Can my child get on the Sleigh

  • Whilst we'd love to spread the magic, unfortunately for safety reasons we can't allow people onto the Sleigh. You're welcome to pose for pictures with Santa - please ask our Elf and Safety staff near the Sleigh before doing so.

Can we donate?

  • Santa is being assisted by the 100th Salford Scout Group and welcomes any and all donations! You can donate cash to any of our collectors with tins, or make a donation online - click Make a Donation in the menu/top of this page

Why don't you cover my street?

  • Salford's a big city! Whilst we'd love to cover all of it, our priority is the streets in the immediate vicinity of our Scout Group (at St Luke's RC Parish Center). Our amazing volunteers help Santa over 10 nights, covering several km in all weathers, so for some streets Santa and his sleigh will only travel a part of the road. For some cul-de-sacs, the Sleigh will pause at the entrance for you to walk up to. We aim to have as many volunteers as possible walking the route and knocking on doors to let you know Santa is approaching.
